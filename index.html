Islands of the Philippines

The Philippine archipelago comprises about 7,600+ islands, of which only about 2,000 are inhabited. More than 5,000 islands of the Archipelago are yet to be named. The Philippine islands are clustered into the three major island groups of Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao.
