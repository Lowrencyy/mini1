APO ISLAND

NEGROS OCCIDENTAL PHILIPPINES

Apo Island is a small, picturesque island located in the Philippines, specifically in the southeastern region of Negros Oriental. It is renowned for its stunning underwater landscapes, rich marine biodiversity, and efforts in marine conservation. Apo Island is situated approximately 7 kilometers (4.3 miles) off the coast of Negros Oriental in the Visayas region of the Philippines

NEGROS OCCIDENTAL,PHILIPPINES

Danjugan Island provides a unique opportunity to immerse oneself in nature and experience firsthand the importance of marine and environmental conservation. Guests can participate in activities such as bird watching, forest trekking, snorkeling, and wildlife observation.celebrated for its commitment to biodiversity conservation and environmental education.

GUIMARAS ISLAND

GUIMARAS PHILIPPINES

The primary attraction of Masungi Georeserve is its stunning karst landscape. Karst refers to a geological formation characterized
by the dissolution of limestone, resulting in unique rock formations, sinkholes, and caves.
The reserve is home to a variety of karst formations, including limestone peaks, karst hills, and limestone chambers.

GIGANTES ISLAND

ILOILO CITY,PHILIPPINES

Gigantes Islands, often referred to as Islas de Gigantes or simply Gigantes, is a group of islands located off the northern coast of the province of Iloilo in the Philippines. These islands are renowned for their stunning natural beauty, pristine beaches, and unique rock formations.
