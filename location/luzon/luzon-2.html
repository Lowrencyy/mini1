DISCOVER LUZON

Luzon is the largest and most populous island in the Philippines, located in Southeast Asia. It is one of the country's three main geographical divisions, with the other two being Visayas and Mindanao. Luzon is known for its diverse landscapes, which include mountains, plateaus, plains, and coastal areas.

TAAL VOLCANO

BATANGAS PHILIPPINES

Taal Volcano is known for its complex geological structure. It is actually a caldera within a caldera. The main caldera, which is a large volcanic crater, contains Taal Lake, while the smaller caldera within it contains the volcanic island called Volcano Island. Within Volcano Island, there is a crater lake known as the Main Crater Lake, which contains a small island called Vulcan Point.

MOUNT PINATUBO

ZAMBALES,PHILIPPINES

The most significant event associated with Mount Pinatubo is its catastrophic eruption in June 1991. This eruption is considered one of the most powerful volcanic eruptions of the 20th century. It ejected vast amounts of volcanic ash, gas into the atmosphere. The eruption resulted in the formation of a large caldera, which filled with water to create a crater lake known as Lake Pinatubo.

MASUNGI

BARAS,PHILIPPINES

The primary attraction of Masungi Georeserve is its stunning karst landscape. Karst refers to a geological formation characterized
by the dissolution of limestone, resulting in unique rock formations, sinkholes, and caves.
The reserve is home to a variety of karst formations, including limestone peaks, karst hills, and limestone chambers.

SUMAGUING CAVE

SAGADA,PHIIPPINES

Sumaguing Cave, also known as Sumaguing Cave System, is a popular and extensive cave network located in Sagada, a town in the Mountain Province of the Philippines. It is one of the most well-known tourist attractions in Sagada and offers a unique and adventurous underground experience.

RIZAL PARK

MANILA,PHILIPPINES

Rizal Park, also known as Luneta Park, is a historical urban park located in the heart of Manila, the capital city of the Philippines.
It is one of the most iconic and significant parks in the country and holds a special place in Philippine history and culture.

SABANG BEACH

BALER,PHILIPPINES

Sabang is a coastal town and popular tourist destination located on the island of Palawan in the Philippines. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, diverse marine life, and proximity to the UNESCO-listed Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park.
