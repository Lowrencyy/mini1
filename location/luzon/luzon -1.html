DISCOVER LUZON

Luzon is the largest and most populous island in the Philippines, located in Southeast Asia. It is one of the country's three main geographical divisions, with the other two being Visayas and Mindanao. Luzon is known for its diverse landscapes, which include mountains, plateaus, plains, and coastal areas.

VAYANG HILLS

BATANES PHILIPPINES

Vayang is a beautiful coastal village located in the province of Batanes, which is the northernmost province of the Philippines. Batanes is known for its stunning natural landscapes, rugged coastlines, and unique cultural heritage. Vayang, like many other areas in Batanes, offers visitors a glimpse into this picturesque and serene paradise.

MAYON VOLCANO

ALBAY,PHIIPPINES

Mayon Volcano, often referred to simply as Mayon, is an active stratovolcano located in the province of Albay in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. It is one of the most iconic and well-known volcanoes in the country and is renowned for its near-perfect cone shape, which makes it one of the world's most symmetrical volcanoes.
