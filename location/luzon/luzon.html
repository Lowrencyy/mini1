DISCOVER LUZON

Luzon is the largest and most populous island in the Philippines, located in Southeast Asia. It is one of the country's three main geographical divisions, with the other two being Visayas and Mindanao. Luzon is known for its diverse landscapes, which include mountains, plateaus, plains, and coastal areas.

PUERTO PRINSESA

PALAWAN PHILIPPINES

Puerto Princesa is a city located on the island of Palawan in the Philippines. It serves as the capital of the province of Palawan. Puerto Princesa is known for its natural beauty, including stunning beaches, lush forests, and vibrant marine life.

BANAUE RICE FIELD

BANAUE,PHILIPPINES

The Banaue Rice Terraces are a complex system of ancient rice terraces that were carved into the mountainsides of the Ifugao region in northern Luzon, Philippines. They were created by the indigenous Ifugao people over 2,000 years ago

PAGUDPOD BEACH

ILOCOS,NORTE

Pagudpud Beach is a popular tourist destination located in the northernmost part of the Philippines, in the province of Ilocos Norte. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, featuring pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear blue waters, and picturesque landscapes.

ITRAMUROS

MANILA,PHIIPPINES

Intramuros is a historic walled area located in the heart of Manila, the capital city of the Philippines. The name "Intramuros" is derived from Spanish and means "within the walls," reflecting its defining characteristic: a large defensive wall that once surrounded the district.

CALLE CRISOLOGO

VIGAN,PHILIPPINES

Calle Crisologo, also known as Crisologo Street, is a famous and picturesque street located in Vigan City, Ilocos Sur, Philippines. It is renowned for its well-preserved Spanish colonial-era architecture and is a popular tourist destination.

BIG LAGOON

EL NIDO PHILIPPINES

Big Lagoon is a stunning natural attraction located on the island of Palawan in the Philippines. It is one of the notable features of El Nido, a popular tourist destination known for its breathtaking landscapes and crystal-clear waters.
